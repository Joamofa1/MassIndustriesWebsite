Vision and Mission Statement


Vision

Mass Industries Limited strives to be leading Regional Manufacturing and Distribution
Company in the biscuit and confectionary industry. Passionate about biscuits with the drive to grow
and develop our market, through our expertise, innovation and empowered talent.

Mission

MASS INDUSTRIES LIMITED is committed to:
- Develop, manufacture and market safe, wholesome quality biscuit and water products.
- Create a fulfilling workplace environment for our employees built on family-based organizational culture and mutual respect.
- Generate sustainanble earnings over time and create long-time value for all our stakeholders.
(This includes not just our employees, but extends to suppliers, customers, consumer and partners.)
